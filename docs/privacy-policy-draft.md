# Lokal Privacy Policy Draft

This draft is for launch preparation and should be reviewed before publication.

## Overview

Lokal helps people discover and save events in Washington, DC. To run the app, Lokal collects account, preference, event interaction, and venue submission information.

## Information Collected

- Account information: name, username, email, phone number, birthdate, and account type.
- Profile information: bio, event interests, neighborhood interests, private-account preference, and venue profile details when applicable.
- Event activity: saved events, RSVPs, attended events, shared events, and story posts.
- Venue information: venue name, address, website, image URL, description, owner or manager contact information, verification requests, and event submissions.
- Technical information: device/browser details and basic logs needed to operate and troubleshoot the app.

## How Information Is Used

- Create and manage accounts.
- Personalize event recommendations.
- Store saved events, RSVPs, and calendar views.
- Support venue verification and event submission review.
- Improve app reliability, safety, and moderation.
- Respond to support, account deletion, or privacy requests.

## Sharing

Lokal does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers used to operate the app, such as hosting, database, analytics, authentication, and infrastructure providers.

## User Controls

Users can update profile settings, make personal profiles private, and request account deletion from Profile settings.

## Data Retention

Lokal keeps account and event activity information while an account is active. Account deletion requests are reviewed and processed according to operational and legal requirements.
