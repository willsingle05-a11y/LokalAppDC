## Description

Lokal helps people in Washington, DC find events worth leaving the house for.

Browse local events by category, date, neighborhood, venue, and vibe. Save events to your personal calendar, RSVP to plans, share events with friends, and follow venues so the city feels easier to navigate.

For locals, Lokal brings together concerts, live music, happy hours, trivia nights, museum events, sports, performing arts, food and drink, festivals, and nightlife.

For venues, Lokal supports venue profiles, verification requests, and event submissions so local teams can get upcoming events in front of the right audience.
