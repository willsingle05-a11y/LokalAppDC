# Lokal Terms of Service Draft

This draft is for launch preparation and should be reviewed before publication.

## Using Lokal

Lokal is an event discovery and planning app for Washington, DC. Users may browse events, save plans, RSVP, share events, follow venues, and create profiles.

## Accounts

Users are responsible for accurate account information and for keeping access to their account secure.

## Venue Accounts

Venue owners and managers may request verification. Lokal may approve, reject, or remove venue access at its discretion. Event submissions may be reviewed before publication.

## User Content

Users and venues may submit profile information, event information, venue details, and other content. Do not submit content that is illegal, misleading, abusive, infringing, or unsafe.

## Moderation

Lokal may remove content, restrict accounts, or decline submissions that violate these terms, app policies, or safety standards.

## Events and Third-Party Links

Event details may come from venues, public sources, APIs, or user submissions. Users should verify event details, prices, age restrictions, and availability with the venue or ticket provider before attending.

## No Guarantee

Lokal is provided as-is. Event availability, pricing, times, and venue details can change.

## Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion from Profile settings. Lokal will review and process deletion requests according to operational and legal requirements.
